NYFW

Keywords to know for Spring 2017

A reminiscence of your favorite '80s dance flick

  • Ruffles

    A ruffle here, a ruffle there: It's the frilly season. From Jill Stuart to Marissa Webb, this bold, classic and uber-girly detail made a huge statement on the runway.

  • Deconstructed Shirting

    Off-shoulder is still going strong. Carolina Herrera pushed down the collars of her shirts dress to expose the clavicle, while Alexander Wang wrapper his cropped shirts down the runway with boxer shorts.

  • Vertical Stripe

    Vertical stripe matches up with a modern and edge look. Victoria Beckham, Philip Lim and others showed pants, shirts and dresses in vertical stripes.
